OUR STORY

Founded in 2007, The Coffee Collective is to this day 100% owned and run by Peter N. Dupont, Casper Engel Rasmussen and Klaus Thomsen. With several competition awards in the bag, including gold in both the World Barista Championship and World Cup Tasting championships, we continue to explore exciting coffee experiences.

ROASTERY

We roast coffee daily and ship directly to wholesale customers, our own coffee shops, subscribers and web shop customers. And yet, freshly roasted coffee is just a small part of the equation.

JOIN US

Our team is diverse in nationalities, ages and personalities. What unifies The Coffee Collective is our common passion for coffee, teamwork, hospitality and ethical coffee trading.
